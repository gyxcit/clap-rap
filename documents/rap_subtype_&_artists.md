# 3. differences
## French Rap Subgenres

### Classic Hip-hop

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |Typical BPM |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|------------|
| **Basic Rap / Hip-hop** | Foundations of rap, simple and direct flow, verse-chorus structure | Breakbeats, soul/funk samples, scratching | Daily life, self-affirmation, hip-hop culture | IAM, NTM, MC Solaar |~80–120 BPM|
| **Boom bap** | Powerful drums (kick + snare), binary rhythm at 90-100 BPM | Jazz/soul samples, deep bass, old school scratching | Technical rap, punchlines, storytelling | Arsenik, Lunatic, La Cliqua |~85–110 BPM|
| **Conscious Rap** | Engaged and thoughtful lyrics, social/political messages | Varied productions but often stripped down to highlight lyrics | Social justice, system critique, philosophy | Keny Arkana, Rocé, Casey |~80–130 BPM|

### Electronic / Modern

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |Typical BPM |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|------------|
| **Trap** | Fast hi-hats (rolls), heavy 808 bass, tempo 130-170 BPM | Dark synths, snappy snares, minimalist production | Money, streets, success, lifestyle | SCH, PNL, Ninho |~130-160 BPM|
| **Drill** | Aggressive and dark rhythm, 808 slides, tempo ~140 BPM | Sliding bass, minimalist piano, gloomy atmosphere | Urban violence, neighborhood wars, dangerous life | Freeze Corleone, Gazo, Ashe 22 |~140-160BPM|
| **Phonk / Dark trap** | Very dark atmosphere, Memphis rap samples, distorted vocals | Saturated 808s, lo-fi effects, heavy reverb | Nocturnal vibes, dark themes | Nekfeu (certain tracks), La Fève |~130-180 BPM|
| **New Wave** | Mix of modern trap and melodies, autotune, cloud aesthetics | Spacey synths, trap 808s, airy production | Introspection, ambition, emotions | Lomepal, Hamza, Damso |~90-160 BPM|
| **Club Rap** | Dancefloor-oriented, catchy hooks, electro production | Powerful bass, energetic beats, house/techno influence | Party, seduction, energy | Lacrim (certain tracks), Niska |~120-140 BPM|

### Melodic / Atmospheric

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |Typical BPM |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|------------|
| **Cloud rap** | Nonchalant flow, airy vocals, dreamy atmosphere | Ethereal synths, reverb, soft bass | Introspection, melancholy, dreamlike atmospheres | PNL, Lomepal, Columbine |70-100 BPM|
| **Chill rap / Lo-fi** | Slow tempo, minimalist production, laid-back vibe | Jazz/soul samples, vinyl crackle, soft beats | Daily life, relaxation, nostalgia | L'Impératrice (rap), Voyou |~60-100 BPM|
| **Emo rap** | Raw emotions, melancholic themes, emotional autotune | Distorted guitars, trap 808s, sad melodies | Depression, breakups, emotional pain | Sch (certain tracks), Lujipeka |~80-120 BPM|

### Fusion & Experimental

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |Typical BPM |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|------------|
| **Experimental / Alternative Rap** | Unconventional structures, innovative sound research | Eclectic productions, multiple influences | Varied themes, creativity above all | Eddy de Pretto, Lomepal, Columbine |~80-180 BPM|
| **Jazz rap** | Jazz samples, live instruments, musical sophistication | Double bass, brass, jazz piano, organic drums | Culture, consciousness, elevation | MC Solaar, Oxmo Puccino, Hugo TSR |~80-100 BPM|
| **Rap rock / rap metal** | Fusion of distorted guitars and rap, punk/metal energy | Guitar riffs, live drums, rock production | Rage, rebellion, statements | Ärsenik (certain tracks), Svinkels |~90-140 BPM|
| **Deepkho** | Emerging genre mixing UK drill and Moroccan sounds | Derbouka, bendir, oriental flutes on drill beats | Identity, dual culture, neighborhoods | Ashe 22, Morad |~120-130 BPM|

### Aggressive / Street

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|
| **Hardcore Rap** | Aggressive flow, brutal vocals, raw energy | Heavy productions, dark samples, intense tempo | Streets, violence, authenticity, hustle | Mafia K'1 Fry, Rohff, La Fouine |~80-110BPM|

### International Influences

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Sounds | Themes | Notable French Artists |
|----------|-----------------|--------|--------|------------------------|
| **Afrotrap / Afro rap** | Fusion of trap and African rhythms, dance, Afro melodies | Coupé-décalé, afrobeats, African percussion | African culture, party, diaspora | MHD, Niska, Dadju |~110-1390BPM|
| **Grime** | Fast tempo (~140 BPM), choppy and aggressive flow | Aggressive synths, electronic basslines, powerful snares | Confrontation, competition, streets | Nekfeu (influences), Freeze Corleone |~140BPM|

---

**Note:** The boundaries between these subgenres are often blurred, and many artists navigate between several styles throughout their career or even from one track to another.
